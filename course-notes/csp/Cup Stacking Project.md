```
^ means slowly pick up one cup without hitting any of the other cups
r means rotate the cup 180 degrees without hitting any of the other cups
. means put down the cup gently without hitting any of the other cups
>n means move the cup right n steps without hitting any of the other cups
```

```rust
^>1.
^>3.
^>5.
^>7.
^r>2.
^r>4.
^r>6.
^>4.
```
